Our Features

VIN NESIA QR Code Generator offers a comprehensive set of features designed to meet all your QR code needs:

  • Text & URL QR Codes: Convert any text or website URL into a scannable QR code
  • WiFi QR Codes: Share network credentials instantly without typing passwords
  • vCard QR Codes: Create digital business cards with contact information
  • Email & SMS QR Codes: Pre-fill email addresses and text messages
  • Location QR Codes: Share GPS coordinates and location information
  • Event QR Codes: Generate calendar events with all necessary details
  • Cryptocurrency QR Codes: Create payment requests for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and more
  • Social Media QR Codes: Link to your social media profiles
  • Custom Styling: Personalize your QR codes with colors, logos, and different styles
  • Multiple Download Formats: Export as PNG or SVG files for any use case
