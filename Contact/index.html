Frequently Asked Questions

Is the QR Code Generator really free?

Yes, our QR Code Generator is completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or limits on the number of QR codes you can create. We support our service through ethical advertising.

Do you store my QR code data?

Most QR code generation happens locally in your browser, so your data stays with you. We don't store the content you input unless it's necessary for specific features. Check our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Can I use QR codes commercially?

Yes, you can use QR codes generated with our service for both personal and commercial purposes. QR codes themselves are not copyrighted, and you retain full rights to use them however you need.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Please email us at admin@vinnesia.my.id with details about the issue, including your browser, device, and steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots are very helpful!

Can you add a specific feature I need?

We love hearing feature requests! Send your ideas to support@vinnesia.my.id. While we can't implement every request, we carefully consider all suggestions for future updates.
